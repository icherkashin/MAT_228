Lax-Wendroff Fourth-Order method demonstrates significant oscillations near discontinuities, as is predicted by dispersion properties of the method. Also, since it is a linear second order method, Godunov's theorem implies that new extrema will be created, which is exactly what is observed.

Naturally, such oscillations, overshoots and undershoots are non-physical and thus Lax-Wendroff Fourth Order method is not appropriate when the initial conditions are discontinuous.
