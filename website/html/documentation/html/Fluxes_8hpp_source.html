8 /* NOTE:
9  * Flux indexing convention:
10  * flux[i] is flux INTO the i-th cell
11  * -flux[i+1] is flux OUT OF the i-th cell <--> flux[i+1] is flux INTO (i+1)-th cell
12  * The flux balance for i-th cell is thus
13  * flux[i] - flux[i+1]
14  * Thus, the boundary condition for fluxes is:
15  * flux[0] = flux[M]
16  * since 0-th and M-th cells are identical for periodic boundary conditions
